# Generate LinkedIn Post

## Purpose
Create LinkedIn posts about business activities and achievements

## Instructions
1. Review completed tasks from Done/
2. Check Business_Goals.md for messaging alignment
3. Generate engaging post (max 300 words)
4. Include relevant hashtags (3-5)
5. Save to Pending_Approval/LINKEDIN_POST_[timestamp].md
6. Schedule for optimal posting time (10am or 3pm)
7. Never post without approval

## Post Template

## Content Guidelines

### Post Types

**1. Achievement Posts**
- Celebrate milestones
- Share completed projects
- Highlight team wins
- Format: "Excited to share..." or "Proud to announce..."

**2. Insight Posts**
- Share lessons learned
- Industry observations
- Best practices
- Format: "Here's what I learned..." or "Key insight..."

**3. Update Posts**
- Product launches
- New features
- Company news
- Format: "Introducing..." or "We're launching..."

**4. Engagement Posts**
- Ask questions
- Start discussions
- Share opinions
- Format: "What do you think about..." or "Let's discuss..."

### Writing Style

**Hook (First Line):**
- Grab attention immediately
- Use emojis sparingly (1-2 max)
- Ask a question or make a bold statement

**Body:**
- Keep paragraphs short (2-3 lines)
- Use line breaks for readability
- Include specific details
- Tell a story when possible

**Call to Action:**
- End with engagement prompt
- Ask for opinions/experiences
- Invite discussion
- Link to relevant content

### Hashtag Strategy

**Primary (1-2):**
- Industry-specific (#AI #Automation)
- Broad reach (#Tech #Innovation)

**Secondary (2-3):**
- Niche topics (#ProductivityTools #WorkflowAutomation)
- Trending topics (when relevant)

**Avoid:**
- More than 5 hashtags
- Overly generic tags (#Success #Business)
- Irrelevant trending tags

## Optimal Posting Times

**Best Times:**
- Tuesday-Thursday: 10:00 AM or 3:00 PM
- Avoid: Weekends, early mornings, late evenings

**Frequency:**
- Maximum 1 post per day
- Ideal: 3-4 posts per week
- Space posts 24+ hours apart
