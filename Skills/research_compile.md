# Skill: Research & Compile

## Purpose
Gather information on a topic and compile into organized format.

## When to Use
- Market research
- Competitor analysis
- Technology evaluation
- Background research for projects

## Process
1. Define research scope and questions
2. Identify information sources
3. Gather relevant data
4. Organize by theme/category
5. Synthesize findings
6. Add sources and citations

## Output Format
```markdown
# Research Report: [Topic]

**Date:** [YYYY-MM-DD]
**Scope:** [Brief description]

## Research Questions
1. Question 1
2. Question 2

## Findings

### Category 1
- Finding 1
- Finding 2
- Finding 3

### Category 2
- Finding 1
- Finding 2

## Key Insights
1. Insight 1
2. Insight 2
3. Insight 3

## Recommendations
- Recommendation 1
- Recommendation 2

## Sources
- Source 1
- Source 2
```
